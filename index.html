📖 Auction Playbook — Keep This in Mind
💰 Budget Rules
  • • Never spend more than 30–35% of budget on a single player
  • • Keep at least 20% in reserve until last 3 rounds
  • • Always know your max-per-slot number before bidding
  • • If a rival is below 25% budget — bid freely, they can't follow
🎯 Bidding Strategy
  • • Nominate players you don't want early — drain rival budgets
  • • Push rivals who need a role — they MUST buy and will overpay
  • • Let rivals fight over big names — swoop in on overlooked mid-tier
  • • Stars drive panic — use them to drain budgets, not always to win
🧩 Squad Balance
  • • Ideal mix: 4 BAT · 4 BOWL · 2 ALL · 1 WK (adjust per format)
  • • Fill WK first — only a few available, they go fast
  • • ALL-rounders give flexibility — worth a slight premium
  • • Don't pile 5+ batsmen — you'll scramble for bowlers at inflated prices
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • • Emotional bidding — don't chase a player just because rivals want him
  • • Forgetting slots — 5 slots left, 5 players in pool? Every one gets bought expensive
  • • Spending early — best value players often come in middle rounds
  • • Ignoring rivals — always check their budget % before entering a bidding war
